Come Experience the Difference at Hawthorne North Park Apartments (formerly Spring Ridge) in Durham, NC. Our large 2 and 3 bedroom apartments and townhomes come in a classic style or a contemporary style which feature BRAND NEW cabinets, countertops, energy efficient appliances, updated flooring, modern light fixtures and so much more! Our spacious homes offer luxury in the forms of fully-equipped kitchens, full-size washer & dryer connections, wood burning fireplaces, plush floor coverings, over-sized windows, lavish bathrooms, spacious bedrooms with walk-in closets and large private balconies or patios. Our residents enjoy exceptional amenities including an outdoor pool pavilion with poolside grilling, 24-hour fitness center, on-site clubhouse, complimentary gourmet coffee station, executive business center, flat screen TVs for your enjoyment, pet park with agility equipment, Wi-Fi at the pool and clubhouse, clothing care center and playground.
