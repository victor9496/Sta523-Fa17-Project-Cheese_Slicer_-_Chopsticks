First let me say that I am a very low maintenance kind of gal, so some of the stuff I mention here may be a challenge for others, but overall didn't bother me. I moved here in July of 2013. When we found this place it was like a blessing. Ashana in the front office was absolutely delightful and she has continued to be since I moved in. I could email her with any request and it was responded to in a timely manner. My apartment is spacious and I have a walkin closet! I also greatly enjoyed the fact that there is a wood burning fire place in the apt. The appliances weren't brand new, but they were as up to date as I needed. I don't really use dishwashers so it doesn't bother me that it's older. And if I wanted a newer dishwasher, there were units that offered this, but paying more wasn't worth that to me. As far as some of the things that were not so great. The shower head sucked. Absolutely no pressure. But I just went to bed bath and beyond and bought a new one. There's one outlet that is not attached to the wall properly. It's annoying, but generally I just don't use it often. My biggest issue...the bugs!!! Yikes! Let me just be clear...my apartment is NOT roach infested in any way, but sometimes these little monsters pop up out of nowhere. I found that it's usually when I have my windows or patio doors open because the screens don't fit all the way and right after it rains, but generally I might see one or two a month during the warmer months. None during the winter. Also, I am deathly afraid of bugs so this was a bigger deal than it might be to others lol. Anywho, once we sprayed and kept my raid close, I felt more comfortable with it. There aren't any ceiling fans, which I wish I would have paid attention to before I moved in. There is one of those lock things like a hotel room that's supposed to be on the door, but it's not and I never requested it get fixed. I have a regular lock and a deadbolt that can only be locked and unlocked from the inside so I feel safe with that. I have a neighbor who plays music loud sometimes, but it is usually before bed time and doesn't interrupt my work or sleep so I really can't complain. There may be other things, but generally those are my only downfalls to living here. Safety was never an issue. Maybe because I live towards the front, near the leasing office, but I've never questioned my safety here. I typically can get a parking space close to my building, but always close enough. I also don't hear a lot of loud noise nor see police officers. Sometimes I hear kids outside during the day, but it's a family community, so that's expected. The pool near me is great and I frequently use the fitness center. It's small, but it works because I don't want to pay for a gym membership. I really like the office staff. They are always really friendly and helpful. When I told them I was terminating my lease because I graduated and would be wrapping up things in NC, they sent me a card with signatures from everyone in the office telling me congratulations! It was the sweetest thing an apartment complex has ever done. They also gave me a welcome package when I moved in with dishwashing liquid, toilet paper, paper towels, etc. they plan these great events for the residents including parties in the leasing office and pools, breakfast on the go, and celebrations for graduates. They also send out and post the cutest newsletters monthly. Maintenance is also very friendly and helpful. I always know if they've been in my apartment because they leave a note of what they did and when. I would, and have, recommended this apartment complex to others. Generally my experience has been great and I can honestly say that if I wasn't moving out of the state I would stay. By the way, the location is AMAZING. It's conveniently close to everything that I need. And the price is quite reasonable!
