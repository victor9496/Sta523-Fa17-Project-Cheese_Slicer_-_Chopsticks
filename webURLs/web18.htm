I chose this apartment because it was a lot of square footage and the rent was a good price. I had a one bedroom. It was pretty spacious. The sunroom is really nice. It's not a luxury apartment, it's a basic apartment. The carpet seemed a bit worn and it got mysterious stains in them. But the bedroom is very large and even with all my furniture it felt very spacious. The main bedroom closet is a nice size. The linen closet and coat closet are also big. The laundry room is a nice bonus. It provides extra storage and full size washer and dryer. I thought the bathroom was a little small but for one person it's fine. I enjoyed my time there. The complex is quiet, except for people running up and down the stairs. I had occasional noise from my upstairs neighbor, but I felt like the walls were pretty thick. I could hear people from the parking lot pretty clearly. There were only a few party nights though. I felt pretty safe, being a single woman living alone. I never fully explored the amenities, but now there's a brand new gym. I could see inside the because the walls are windows and people are really enjoying it. The rent was nice, the space was nice. I think I would have stayed if it wasn't for the bugs. On the first morning I spent in there, I saw a large tree roach in the hallway. It was my first time seeing one. Then I saw German cockroaches, in the first couple of days, although the apartment had been very clean upon move in. The roaches were an ongoing problem. Nothing terrible but every couple of weeks, I would see one. It was almost like clockwork. I had them spray at least once a month and often more times than that. So I saw a lot of dead roaches but they were still around. Then I would see a live roach. During the summer I had mysterious flying bugs in the place... it seemed like they came through the fireplace. They were like little bees. I saw a worm/centipede thing crawling up the wall from underneath the carpet. I saw a large tree roach crawl in from underneath the door and up the wall. After that, I got a draft blocker. One brown dead roach in my kitchen was the length and width of my thumb. I kept all my food carefully put away at all times. The only time I left a dish in the sink, there was a live roach there to greet me. The complex is in the woods, so I think bugs are probably inevitable. They did get a new pest control company while I was there but I saw little improvement. Sometimes it would take them 2 or 3 weeks to respond to my pest control request. I found the best way to make it happen on time was call them, not request online. Regular maintenance happened very fast if you requested online. If there were less bugs, I would have stayed longer. Other than that, it was a nice place, especially if you really want to get a lower rent in a great area. Very close to food, grocery stores, I-40, the mall. I definitely loved the location.
