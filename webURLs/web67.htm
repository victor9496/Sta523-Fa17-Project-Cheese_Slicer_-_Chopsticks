2017 Recommended Property Reviewed 11/24/2017
This complex has some good and bad points. You get a modern, well constructed apartment with good amenities and good management staff at around a 15% discount off market price. There is a mix of low income renters (retirees, disabled, hood rats) and blue collar workers. It is within a few miles from a shooting range and you'll hear gunshot occasionally in the distance. A wastewater treatment facility is nearby but so far I haven't noticed any bad smells. Management really helped me by rushing my move in as much as they could and helping with disability accommodations. For the price this complex had the best apartments I could find. The area is beautiful. I live on the third floor and an egret flew past my window. Landscaping isn't terrible but there are patches of burned out grass. Maintenance hasn't impressed me that much-they tracked mud into my apartment, some repairs to a mesh screen and cabinet resurfacing are falling apart and they get backed up. Ghetto hood rats are a source of irritation with their public fighting, shouting across the parking lot, drug use, screaming kids, litter and crime. I have had to talk to neighbors two times in my first week to ask them to keep the noise down after 10 pm and control their loud kids. They have responded so it looks manageable. My car was broken into the second night at the complex. The pictures was taken in Dec 2017 of a recent wheel theft (it even had locking wheel nuts!) Management needs to do a better job screening applicants, enforce the lease provisions for noise around the complex and put some kind of monitoring in the parking lot to deter crime. There is no place to lock bikes or charge electric cars. Very basic nonprogrammable thermostats inside. Some of the amenities aren't modern but they are upgrading. I've been told that my water heater system will be upgraded to a tankless one within a few months. Good double panned insulated windows, quality blinds. Impressive large size bathtub, high ceilings, crown moulding and professional interior decorator color scheme. The amenities go a long way to mitigating problems. For example, thick windows help keep down outside noise. Wall thickness seems fine and I can't hear my immediate neighbors at normal noise levels.

I lived at Falls Pointe for 4 years and honestly had zero complaints. The problem with these review boards is that you mostly get comments from people that either love a place or hate a place. For my part, I lived in a building with fantastic neighbors, one of whom was an onsite maintenance man that was always there if I needed help and the others were neighbors who became friends. I had a 2bed/2bath on the second floor. My electricity bill was usually between $50-70 depending on season. Water, trash, and sewer were included. There was hardly ever any noise from above or below me, which either indicates considerate neighbors or good insulation between floors; probably a little of both. I had one cat, and whenever the maintenance team was in the apartment they were always considerate enough to make sure she did not get out. Pros: Onsite maintenance guys were always nice, knew my name, and fixed any maintenance issues within a day or two. The only issues were non-emergency so that is pretty quick. They have golf carts to respond quickly to any issue. I had a garage, so I did not use the outside parking much, but when I did there were always open spots near my door. If garages are important to you, mine was huge by comparison to the one in the apartment complex that I moved to (out of state). The garage at Falls Pointe held my car, motorcycle, several boxes, a cabinet and other items, and I was always able to walk around my car. My current garage is at least 30% smaller. The apartment grounds were always clean when I was outside and well maintained. I always felt safe walking in the community. The office staff, especially Tracy, knew my name and always treated me with respect, and I like to think that I returned the favor. Overall, the complex was great for me. It was affordable, near enough to several strip malls, grocery stores, and restaurants to be convenient, but tucked back away from those things....enough that it was quiet and I could get work done from home when I needed to. It was also close to my office, so the savings in gas mileage was a nice bonus. Cons: None I would highly recommend this community to anyone.
