I've lived here just over 6 months and have found it disappointing. Don't be fooled by the model home they show you; I was told my unit would look just like the renovated apartment I was shown and it certainly does not. It looks every one of its years, with chipped paint on the walls, peeling paint on the ceiling, and obvious water marks from previous leaks. On move in I found that the "thorough cleaning and inspection" I had to wait to move in for had apparently not happened. Surfaces and blinds were filthy, carpet was full of dust and pet hair, paint splatters everywhere, toilet seat broken, there was an odor as if the last occupant had smoked, etc. It seems like things break every month and I'm constantly making maintenance requests. On multiple occasions in the summer I had HUGE cockroaches in my unit and on my balcony despite keeping the unit clean. I don't know how they track who's picking up packages because I haven't received a slip in months and write my entry in their book when I walk in the door and take it myself. With regard to public areas, there is dog waste everywhere. Common areas like stairs and corridors in the apartment buildings are left dirty. Part of that is neighbors who don't clean up after themselves when their garbage spills and part of it is just poor management of the property. In the recent snowstorm, there was no grit salt put down ahead of time and walks were left unshoveled so every surface was covered in dangerous ice until it melted naturally. In the summer, the pool had a broken ladder with rusted screws at the bottom, and gave me a rash; it was closed before Labor Day for those who could use it. Broken-down cars are allowed to just sit there in the lots for weeks on end. The complex trash and recycling areas are a dumping ground for anything from toilets and furniture to broken appliances. There are issues with neighbors and safety. I have had to knock on a neighbor's door multiple times because of how loud their music is, and they came to the door obviously high. There are often groups of young men loitering and I've had to call the police because I could hear a domestic disturbance in the next building over. To top it all off there was a shooting death here in September 2016 of a 16-year-old who broke into a unit, which we received an email about describing it as a "firearms discharge" a few days later - quite a difference between a discharge and a death! We've received no updates since then. No visible action has been taken to secure the community, like fixing the main gates that have never been closed (which might help with the dumping issue as well). Look elsewhere.
