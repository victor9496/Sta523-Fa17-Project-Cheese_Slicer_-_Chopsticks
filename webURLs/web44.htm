The Murchison apartments run by Trinity Properties was a great place to live over 5 years. I enjoyed every single thing about living in the Murchison apartments, except the large yearly rent increases, which is the reason for 4 instead of 5 stars. However, even with the large increases, they are very consistent and straightforward about how they calculate them. The company: Everyone I have dealt with in the offices and in maintenance have all been wonderful throughout my time there, really the best in any apartment setting throughout my experiences in Durham and Asheville. They are very responsive whenever there are repairs that need to be done, flexible if you have pets, and friendly & respectful during all interactions. They are supportive of residents, in terms of crime prevention (hosting police events where you can get your valuables registered, sharing information about suspicious activities), community building (there are usually a couple of events every year), and responding to any concerns you have. The Murchison apartments: The apartments are big, have new appliances, and are well maintained and well constructed. The green space/shrubbery by the buildings is also well maintained (and you can plant things if you want - I had tomato plants and herbs). Utilities (gas, electric, water) never cost that much and cell phone reception was good (I have AT&T). I read some other reviews mentioning problems with bugs. I never had this, but they do stop by to treat several times per year, and with cats, I have never had bug issues. Location: It's a great location, walking or biking distance to the Rigsbee area, downtown Durham, or Duke east campus. You're within a mile of a dog park, the Ellerbee Creek trail is a block away, I-85 is close, the bus line picks up at the end of the block, and the neighborhood is really friendly and open. I never felt unsafe in the neighborhood, either walking or biking, and I definitely felt safe at home. Also, it's very easy to find on street parking, and there is off street parking which I used when I was leaving town. I think this is a fantastic place to live if you plan to just stay a short time (a few years or less) or if you are able to afford much more than the current rent price. Definitely a great company to rent from.
