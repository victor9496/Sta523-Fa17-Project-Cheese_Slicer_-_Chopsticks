I have generally enjoyed my time at the Belmont. The staff in the office and maintenance are great, and the community is very well maintained. Amenities are also high quality - the pool is great during the summer, the gym is always clean though, while large for an apartment community, is somewhat small, and the running trail around the pond is a great feature. I also appreciated an in-unit laundry room with W/D provided. The community is also very pet friendly; my 60 lb dog has had a pleasant stay, though I always wonder why apartments feel inclined to charge an additional pet rent. Regarding the community, perhaps the only negative I could offer is that the gate is often not working or under repair, reducing the appeal of this feature. While the community in general is great, I would say the apartments themselves are probably a bit dated. Appliances appear older, counter tops aren't of a great quality and are sometimes hard to clean, and the rugs seem to come up a bit easily. I also had a bit of a surprise when I discovered I was assigned a handicap accessible apartment without my knowledge upon move-in. This wasn't a big deal to me, but the floor plan was different than the model and what was presented when I was first considering the complex, so definitely make sure you ask before move-in day. I do think if this complex were located elsewhere (not so close to Duke), it would probably be priced lower, so keep in mind that you're paying for proximity to campus. This also means that there is a sizable student population. I'm not a student (employee at Duke), and this didn't bother me at all; I had no complaints about noise or anything else really, but this might be important to some. So, in summary: -Pros: staff are prompt and courteous community is well-kept amenities are great and well maintained close to Duke, several restaurants, bus stop, and Kroger pet friendly W/D in unit Generally quiet -Cons: apartments are outdated paying for location the gate is often under repair pet rent in addition to pet fee upfront
